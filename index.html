About

I moved from Rochester, NY to Chicago, IL in 1987 and never left except for college. I received my BFA in Graphic Design from Rhode Island School of Design in 1994. Recently, I completed the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Northwestern University with a 4.0 grade average. I am a problem-solver by nature; all aspects of art and design interest me. I took my design skills to Michelin-starred kitchens of Chicago and was a Pastry Chef for a 5-year sabbatical, but now I’m back and hungry for design, not cake!

After art, design, and food (and the Oxford comma), my next-favorite subject is my dog, Milton. He’s a 14 year-old whippet; my family has always had sighthounds‚Äîmainly whippets, although I adopted a greyhound when I was in college. Our dogs are always named with a literary reference. Milton has an international fan club. To stay current with his pampered lifestyle, check out his Facebook page. I’m also an avid gardener, world traveler, clothes horse, and Sunday New York Times crossword puzzle-doer.
